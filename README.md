### Story in a nutshell

You wake up in an unknown world after dying, where someone with a Claymore and a grenade running around is a "Common" occurence, not the 'running around' part, but the claymore and the grenade part. In this world techology advanced so fast, that the steps blended together, in this world magic and alchemy exist, and live together with science and technology. You have multiple choice in this world:

1. Try to **rule the world**.
1. Try to **find your way back** to your world.
1. Be the **Hero** of this world
1. Live in the **shadows**.
